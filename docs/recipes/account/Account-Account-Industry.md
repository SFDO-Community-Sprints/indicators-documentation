## Account - Account Industry

### Description


> Show different icons depending on the Type of Account

### Fields

| Fields | Value | Description |
|-----------|-----------|--------------------------|
|sObject|`Account`
|Field|` Account Industry`|
|Description|`Shows primary industries that the company is interested in`
